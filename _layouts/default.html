Projects

FYP Project: Muar-in-Motion

Muar-in-Motion, initiated by The Muara (FG 21) under IIUM's Flagship Project in collaboration with KLM and Tanjung Kunang Enterprise, aims to boost the tourism industry in Muar. The project focuses on developing an intuitive web application offering essential information on tourist attractions, transportation, and accommodation. The platform also facilitates seamless communication among visitors and provides a well-organized event page for trip planning.

Blockchain Voting dApp

The election and voting system, crucial for democratic processes, faces challenges like fraud and data tampering. To address these issues, a blockchain-based voting dApp is proposed. By leveraging blockchain's security features, the dApp aims to enhance transparency, security, and tamper-resistance, providing a decentralized infrastructure to mitigate risks of manipulation and ensuring a more trustworthy and democratic electoral experience.

Healthcare System

HealthWeb Systems assists a newly opened clinic in implementing a web-based registration system, replacing manual registration to improve patient tracking and service management. Aligned with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 3 and 9, the system aims to enhance health coverage, provide access to medicines, and contribute to technological progress for a more interconnected and prosperous clinic.

Donation Hub

The project aims to create a website, "Donation Hub," centered around the theme of "Organisation" to address Sustainable Development Goals such as No Poverty, Zero Hunger, and Good Health and Well-being. The organized flow includes a homepage with project goals, a "Catalogue" for item donations, a "Volunteer" page for registrations, a "News" page for charity events, and a "Contact Us" page for inquiries and communication channels.
